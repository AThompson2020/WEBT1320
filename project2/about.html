Introduction to Witchcraft

You may be wondering, "What do witches need to know? What do they do? How can I become one?" First off, a witch controls the elements, heals creatures of the Earth, and communes with the dead to help them reach their final destination. A witch needs self control, wisdom, and a sprinkle of magic to bring it all together. If you wish to become a witch, just follow our guide and grab some witchy necessities on your way out.
