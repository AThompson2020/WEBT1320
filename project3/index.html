Forever Brew

Step 1

Gather ingredients for your forever brew. Ingredients: Water, Eye of Newt, Frog's legs, Dead man's toe, and molar of a priest.

Step 2

Boil your ingredients inside your caldron.

Step 3

Find a child. Any child will do but a misbehaving one is always more fun. Then, pour your potion into a mug, top with marshmellows, and serve to the child.

Step 4

Child will then turn into candy. Eat the candy and live forever! Go on to take over the world! *Cue Maniacal laughter*
